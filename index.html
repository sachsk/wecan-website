What is WECAN Foundation

("World Environment and Climate Action Network")
In last some years, we are facing effects of Climate change in terms of extreme weather events like drought, floods and cyclones. Day by day, the frequency and intensity of these issues is increasing. According to IPCC report of 2018, we have only 12 year remaining to reach Earth mean temperature rise of 1.5 degree Celsius. After reaching 1.5 degree Celsius, The frequency and intensity of extreme weather events will be very high. It will also result, starting many irreversible processes and positive feedback loops, so it will be impossible for us to save the humanity. So "WECAN foundation" believe that, we all need to start journey towards becoming 'Carbon Neutral'. We all can reduce our carbon footprint by reducing our consumption and planting trees.
"WECAN foundation", is planning to approach this issue by below three ways
1. Aware as much as people about the seriousness and urgency of this issue.
2. Take action with people to absorb the carbon and and make as much as people carbon neutral.
3. Start the, sustainable, low carbon, decentralized businesses.
So my dear friends, put your hand to increase this network...
"we all together let's built a network to save our future"...!!!!
"If Not now never then"...!!!!
